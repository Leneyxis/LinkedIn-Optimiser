Take a Full Screenshot

Please follow the steps below to take a full-page screenshot of your LinkedIn profile and upload it for optimization:

For Chrome Browser:

  • Press F12 (Windows) or Cmd + Option + I (macOS) to open the Developer Tools.
  • Click the three dots (⋮) in the top-right corner of the Developer Tools panel.
  • Click on Run Command > Type Screenshot and choose Capture full-size screenshot.

Manual Method (All Browsers):

  • Scroll to the top of the LinkedIn profile page.
  • Use your system’s screenshot tool to take screenshots while scrolling down.
